Getting Started with GoalForge

How do I create my first goal?

+

To create your first goal:

  1. Go to the Goals page from the navigation
  2. Click the "Create New Goal" button
  3. Fill in your goal title and description
  4. Add milestones to break down your goal
  5. Set a deadline (optional)
  6. Click "Save Goal"

What are Circles and how do they work?

+

Circles are small groups (up to 5 people) where you can:

  • Share your goals and progress
  • Get encouragement from friends
  • Stay accountable with weekly check-ins
  • Celebrate achievements together

To join or create a circle, go to the Circles page.

How does the streak system work?

+

Your streak increases when you complete your weekly check-in. The system tracks:

  • Weekly Check-ins: Complete each week to maintain your streak
  • Goal Progress: Working on goals also contributes to motivation
  • Consistency Bonus: Longer streaks unlock special achievements

Goals & Progress Tracking

How do I add milestones to my goals?

+

When creating or editing a goal, you can add milestones by:

  1. Clicking the "Add Milestone" button
  2. Entering a description for each milestone
  3. Setting due dates for individual milestones (optional)
  4. Reordering milestones by dragging them

Each completed milestone increases your overall goal progress automatically.

Can I edit or delete a goal after creating it?

+

Yes! You can manage your goals by:

  • Editing: Click on any goal to view details, then click "Edit"
  • Deleting: In the goal details view, click "Delete" (this cannot be undone)
  • Archiving: Mark completed goals as archived to clean up your active goals list

How is progress calculated?

+

Progress is automatically calculated based on:

  • Milestone Completion: Each milestone contributes equally to the total progress
  • Manual Updates: You can manually adjust progress if needed
  • Time-based: If deadlines are set, progress may include time-based calculations

Circles & Friends

How do I invite friends to my circle?

+

To invite friends to join your circle:

  1. Go to the Circles page
  2. Select your circle or create a new one
  3. Click "Invite Members"
  4. Share the invitation link with your friends
  5. They can join using the link

Note: Free accounts can have up to 5 members per circle.

What can circle members see?

+

Circle members can see:

  • Your active goals and progress
  • When you complete milestones
  • Your weekly check-in status
  • Messages you send to the circle

You can adjust privacy settings in your Settings → Privacy page.

How do weekly check-ins work with circles?

+

Weekly check-ins help maintain accountability:

  • Each week, you'll be prompted to complete a check-in
  • Circle members can see who has completed their check-in
  • Completed check-ins increase your streak
  • You can send encouragement to members who haven't checked in yet

Account & Settings

How do I change my email or password?

+

Currently, email addresses cannot be changed for security reasons. For password changes:

  1. Go to Settings → Account
  2. Look for the password change section
  3. Enter your current and new password
  4. Save your changes

If you need to change your email, please contact support.

Can I export my data?

+

Yes! You can export all your GoalForge data:

  1. Go to Settings → Data & Storage
  2. Click "Export My Data"
  3. Your data will be downloaded as a JSON file

This file contains all your goals, circles, progress, and settings.

How do I delete my account?

+

To permanently delete your account:

  1. Go to Settings → Data & Storage
  2. Click "Delete Account"
  3. Confirm your decision in the popup

Warning: This action cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently deleted.

Troubleshooting

I forgot my password. How can I reset it?

+

If you've forgotten your password:

  1. Go to the login page
  2. Click "Forgot Password"
  3. Enter your email address
  4. Check your email for reset instructions

If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact support.

The app is running slow. What can I do?

+

Try these troubleshooting steps:

  • Clear cache: Go to Settings → Data & Storage → Clear Cache
  • Check internet connection
  • Update your browser to the latest version
  • Disable browser extensions that might interfere
  • Try a different browser

My progress isn't saving. What should I do?

+

If your progress isn't saving:

  1. Check your internet connection
  2. Make sure you're logged in (check the header)
  3. Try refreshing the page
  4. Clear your browser cache
  5. Contact support if the issue persists
